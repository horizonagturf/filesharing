# Getting started

This guide walks you through signing in and sharing your first bundle of files.

## Sign in

Most organizations use **Sign in with Microsoft**. Click the button on the login page and authenticate with your work account.

If your organization uses username and password instead, enter your credentials on the login page.

## Your bundles

After signing in, the homepage lists your existing bundles grouped by status (draft, pending approval, active, and expired). Click **Open** on a bundle to continue editing it, or **Create new upload** to start fresh.

## The upload wizard

Creating a share takes three steps:

1. **Settings** — Title, description, expiry, optional password, share mode, and recipients.
2. **Files** — Drag and drop files or browse your computer. You can add more files later before completing the bundle.
3. **Links** — After you finish, you receive preview and download links (or invitations are sent to recipients).

Click **Complete** when all files are uploaded and settings look correct. Depending on your organization's policy, the bundle may be sent immediately or held for approval.

## Tips

- Give each bundle a clear title so recipients know what they are receiving.
- Set an expiry date for sensitive files rather than leaving them available indefinitely.
- You can return to a draft bundle from the homepage at any time before completing it.
